Plane–Point Distance Calculator

Compute the perpendicular distance from a point to a plane in 3D space

Define the plane by three points P1, P2, P3 lying on it. Enter the free point P4 whose distance to the plane you want.

P1 — first point on the plane
P2 — second point on the plane
P3 — third point on the plane
P4 — free point (distance target)

Enter the plane as ax + by + cz = d, then the free point P.

Plane equation coefficients
P — free point (distance target)

Enter the plane in Normaleform: a normal vector n and a point Q on the plane. The plane is the set of all x with (x − Q) · n = 0.

n — normal vector of the plane
Q — a point on the plane
P — free point (distance target)
